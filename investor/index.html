About MATAAI

Built on Trust, Driven by Precision

MATAAI NIGERIA LIMITED was founded with a single purpose: to give Nigerian businesses and global trade partners a logistics company they can truly rely on. From our base in Port Harcourt, we have grown into a nationally recognised freight forwarder with deep expertise across every shipping mode.

Our team of certified professionals handles every shipment as if it were their own cargo — with urgency, care, and full regulatory compliance at every stage. We don't just move freight; we build long-term partnerships based on transparency, reliability, and results.

Reliability

We deliver on our promises. If it's booked, it moves — on time and in full.

Compliance

Every shipment handled in full accordance with Nigerian and international trade regulations.

Transparency

Real-time updates and honest communication throughout the entire journey.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards so your business never has to compromise.
